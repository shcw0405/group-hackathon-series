About This Edition

The inaugural DSL Hackathon will be held within the research group, focusing on how to leverage generative AI tools — such as Claude Code, NotebookLM, Nano Banana, OpenClaw, and Agent Skills — to enhance research efficiency and productivity.

Participants may join individually or form teams of up to three members. Over the course of two weeks, teams will explore and apply cutting-edge AI tools to solve real research challenges, from data analysis and literature review to prototype development and experimental automation.
