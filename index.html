What is DSL Hackathon Series

Build Fast. Learn Deep. Ship Real.

A multi-format hackathon program designed to help students master modern AI tools, build real products, and turn ideas into prototypes — from 24-hour sprints to 4-week innovation cycles.

01

Build with AI

Learn and apply cutting-edge tools like Claude Code, AI-assisted development workflows, and emerging research tools to rapidly turn ideas into working prototypes.

02

Solve Real Problems

Work on meaningful challenges — from research ideas to product concepts — and develop solutions that go beyond demos.

03

Learn by Shipping

Move beyond theory. Gain hands-on experience in coding, prototyping, iteration, and deployment through real project cycles.

04

Grow as a Team

Collaborate in fast-paced environments, practice communication, and experience what it's like to build as a real startup team.
